VIBHU Hotels and Villas Cookie Policy

LAST REVISED ON December 12th, 2022

 

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the VIBHU Hotels and Villas Hotel and its Affiliates and Subsidiaries (“we” or “us”) collects, uses, consults or otherwise processes an individual’s Personal Data regarding the usage of Cookies. This Privacy Policy applies globally but depending on where you live some specific provisions of this Privacy Policy may not apply to you.

 

Cookies Management Tool

As VIBHU Hotels and Villas  respects your right to privacy, we provide you with a Cookies Management Tool to help you to manage your cookies preferences including providing consent or ensuring minimum operability of the website you have chosen to visit. In the latter case, we collect and use cookies on all the company’s websites (“the Service”). Our website uses cookies to distinguish your session or if you consent thereon, yourself e.g. in the case of a login session at your VIBHU Hotels and Villas  account, from other sessions or other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site. By clicking to accept the usage of cookies after having taken into account the different category headings so as to find out more and change our default settings (except for strictly necessary cookies, the usage of cookies on this website is deactivated by default) you consent to the use of cookies. However, blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of the site and the services we are able to offer.

Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third-parties we may partner with may use cookies on the Service and your choices regarding cookies.

 

What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser or by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows us or a third-party, made known to you, to recognise you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies.

 

How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • to enable certain functions of the Service,
  • to provide analytics,
  • to store your preferences,
  • to enable advertisements delivery.

 

– Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work.

 

– Performance cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor its performance.

 

What are your choices regarding cookies?

Tracking technology helps us manage and improve the usability of the VIBHU Hotels and Villas  Website, for example by detecting whether there has been any contact between your computer and us in the past and to identify the most popular sections of the VIBHU Hotels and Villas’ Website.

 

Based on a more detailed description on the usage of cookies our Cookies Management Tool (herein “the Tool”) helps you to manage your cookies preferences including providing consent. You can withdraw your consent to the usage of certain cookies any time using the aforementioned Tool.

When you save your cookie settings, they should also apply to your future visits to the VIBHU Hotels and Villas  Website. However, for technical reasons beyond the control of VIBHU Hotels and Villas , this cannot be guaranteed. For example, if you reset your browser, delete your cookies or access the VIBHU Hotels and Villas  Website from another browser or device, your cookie settings may be not be retrieved. To comply with applicable laws and regulations, in some countries you may be asked to confirm your cookie settings when you first visit the VIBHU Hotels and Villas  Website. If you are in a country where you are automatically required to set your cookie settings, you may be asked to set them again on a future visit.

Please ensure that your browser setting reflects whether you wish to pre-exclude tracking technologies (such as cookies). The specific capabilities of your browser and instructions on how to use them can usually be found in the manual or help file of your browser.

Without tracking technologies the availability of the services provided by the VIBHU Hotels and Villas   Website may be reduced, or parts of the VIBHU Hotels and Villas Website may no longer function correctly.

Questions, comments, remarks, requests or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy are welcome and should be addressed to info@vibhuhotelsandvillas.com. If you have questions about an individual hotel’s practices or the information it has, please contact the hotel directly.
